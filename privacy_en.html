Privacy Policy

EZDN Player

EZDN Player values your privacy and prioritizes the protection of your personal information. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

EZDN Player does not process personal information for the following reasons:

  • This app is purely a local music player
  • There is no user account system
  • There is no data communication with servers
  • No external advertising or analytics tools are used

2. Information We Collect

EZDN Player does not collect any personal information.

2.1 Local Data Access

The app only accesses the following local data, which is processed solely on your device:

  • Downloads Folder: Access MP3 files you've downloaded to add them to your playlist
  • Clipboard: Access clipboard to apply copied images as album art (only when you explicitly long-press and select paste)
  • Music Files: Access MP3 files on your device for playback

Important: All of the above data is processed only on your device and is never transmitted to or stored on external servers.

3. Processing and Retention Period

EZDN Player does not collect personal information, so there is no personal information to retain or process.

All information you set within the app (playlists, album art, metadata edits, etc.) is stored only on your device and will be deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

EZDN Player does not provide your personal information to third parties. The app works completely without an internet connection and does not communicate with external servers.

5. External Service Links

EZDN Player provides links to the following external services:

  • YouTube Search: You can navigate to the YouTube website to search for songs if you choose
  • Album Art Image Search: You can navigate to search engines to find album art images if you choose

When using these external services, the privacy policies of those services apply. EZDN Player is not responsible for data processing on external services.

6. Permission Usage

EZDN Player requests the following permissions:

  • File Access Permission: Required to access the Downloads folder and music files on your device

All permissions are used only to provide core app functionality. If you deny permission, you will not be able to use the corresponding features.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

EZDN Player is safe for all ages, including children under 14. Since the app does not collect any personal information, there are no concerns about children's privacy protection.

8. Privacy Officer

EZDN Player will sincerely respond to inquiries related to the protection of your personal information.

  • Email: metyasoft@gmail.com
  • Response Time: Inquiries will be answered within 7 days

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be added to, deleted, or modified in accordance with changes in laws, policies, or security technologies. If the privacy policy changes, we will notify you through app updates.

10. Additional Information

10.1 Data Security

All data is stored on your device and protected by iOS's sandbox security model, which prevents access from other apps or external sources.

10.2 Data Deletion

When you delete the app, all data created within the app (playlists, settings, etc.) is automatically deleted. Original music files you added will not be deleted.

10.3 Transparency

EZDN Player prioritizes your trust. If you have any questions about how the app works or how data is processed, please feel free to contact us at any time.

Summary: EZDN Player does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information, and all data is processed only on your device. The app works completely without an internet connection and guarantees 100% privacy.
